Components which can be Autometed

Sensors

Water Level Control Sensors are installed on the Overhead water Tank and Underground Sump.
Once installed, they keep sending data about water level in both Overhead Tank and Underground Sump to the main Controller.

Controller

Controller is the brain of the Smart Water Level Controller.
It connects to the Internet through your Home WiFi network.
It monitors Water Level in Overhead Tank and Underground Sump continuously in real time and automatically turns on/off Water Pump based on the Water Levels.

Mobile App

Mobile App is available for Android OS for Smart Phones.
Through this Mobile App, you can Monitor and Control the Water Level in Overhead Tank and Underground Sump, from anywhere in the world; using the Internet.
